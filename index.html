Somerville Community Language Center

Our Mission Statement

Somerville Community Language Center is a non-profit whose mission is to provide free language courses to all members of the Somerville community. We wholeheartedly celebrate the great diversity of languages that are spoken in Somerville. The city of Somerville reports that "more than 50 languages are spoken in Somerville schools." Language is a beautiful way to connect with others.
